Refund Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with our products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund under the conditions outlined below.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are generally available for purchases made within the last 14 days if:

  • The software does not function as described.
  • You feature-breaking bugs that we are unable to fix in a reasonable timeframe.
  • You purchased the wrong license by mistake.

3. Non-Refundable Circumstances

We may decline refund requests if:

  • The request is made more than 14 days after purchase.
  • The software was used for unauthorized or illegal purposes.
  • The issue is caused by third-party platform changes (e.g., if Telegram blocks the method entirely), though we will make every effort to restore functionality first.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@codeaeternum.com with your Order ID and the reason for the refund request.

5. Processing Time

Refunds are processed by our payment provider, Paddle.com. Once approved, funds usually return to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.
